FocusPrompter

Start every day
with intention.

A personal Slack companion that helps you plan your day, track what matters, and stay focused on the work that counts.

☀

Morning Planning

Start each day with a clear view of what's pending, what's stuck, and what you carried over from yesterday.

↻

Carryover Tracking

Tasks don't disappear. If something's been sitting for 3+ days, you'll know it's time to act or let go.

🎯

Refocus Anytime

Lost in the weeds? One command brings you back to your priorities and asks: what's the smallest next step?

💬

Lives in Slack

No new app to open. Manage tasks right where you already work, through simple DM commands.

Simple Commands

add [task] Add a new task to your list
list See all pending tasks
done [id] Mark a task complete
focus Start morning planning
refocus Get back on track
